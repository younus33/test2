Technology

Nucleon developed its own technology for collecting and analyzing cyber security threats. Nucleon built a framework which ocnsists of tools that helps organizations to implement threat intelligence easily. Nucleon is only examining cyber attacks that were collected by its own sensors which makes its intelligence unique and with low false positives rate.



Sensors

Nucleon operates hundreds of globally distributed polymorphic sensors to monitor the Internet and to detect and learn from known and unknown attacks. Nucleon’s Polymorphic Sensors may be considered best as the natural evolution of advanced honeypots. Each polymorphic sensor is crafted to look and feel exactly like the real thing, whether it mimics a bank, municipality or airport. A polymorphic sensor is aimed to look like a real network with some vulnerabilities waiting to be exploited. The polymorphic technology that allows each sensor to change or replace its locations as well as the type of network it mimics, to protect their real identity from being revealed.

Deep Learning analysis

All the data that is being collected by the sensors is transferred into NANN. Advanced machine learning technologies are used for further in-depth analysis and verification of attacks and malware. All analyzed data collected autonomously by the polymorphic sensors are forwarded and saved to the NANN central brain, automatically and in real time. The data is stored in multiple databases and cross referenced in different ways using different algorithms, to search for possibly hidden relations between attacks and actors. This is all done completely autonomously, resulting with a list of live indicators of cyber threats.

Actionable Intelligence

The main purpose of the NANN is to predict and issue early warnings about upcoming attacks. The NANN is integrated into the Nucleon Proactive Defense System, and is set to provide 24x7 real time actionable intelligence. Precise information about for example botnets, APTs, DDoS, attack sources and malicious URLs/IPs. The Nucleon intelligence feed compliments your security measures and can be fully integrated with your SIEM and other existing security and defense systems.

Non-intrusive

The Nucleon Artificial Neural Network is an autonomous, standalone Cloud service operated by Nucleon that only monitors what is going on in the outside of your firewall. The NANN does not monitor or reside within the client’s internal network or systems.

Counter Intelligence

Do you question whether malicious actors are already inside your network? Using the same technology and methodology to monitor the outgoing traffic we can detect and identify indicators of malicious actors or malware that are already inside your network.
